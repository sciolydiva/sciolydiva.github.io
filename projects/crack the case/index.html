"Crack the Case"

4/15/23 - In person activity at Peggy Thomsen Park

Calling all detectives! In our forensics event, we presented students with unique stations that applied forensic techniques. Kids learned about ciphers, chromatography, fingerprinting, and even got to play with microscopy. The event culminated in an all-out investigation to crack the case of The Missing Slime Recipe!
