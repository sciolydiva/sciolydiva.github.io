Volcanoes

Summer 2022 Slideshow Project

In this project, students were invited to create their own mini play dough volcanoe! They used baking soda and dishsoap to cause the eruption. They also learned about the different types of volcanoes and how they are formed! Follow along with this slideshow to learn more about volcanoes and recreate your own!
